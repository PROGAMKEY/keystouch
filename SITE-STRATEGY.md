# Key's Touch — Website Redesign Strategy

## The Problem with the Current Site
- Positioned as a generic dev shop ("we build apps")
- No clear authority positioning
- Weak conversion paths — single "Book Consultation" CTA
- No social proof beyond 4 generic testimonials
- No lead capture, no nurture funnel
- Doesn't communicate premium pricing or high-value outcomes

## New Positioning
**From:** "We build apps and websites"
**To:** "We engineer AI-powered systems that eliminate manual work and scale revenue"

**Tagline:** "Automate Everything. Scale Anything."

**Target Clients:**
- Mid-market companies ($5M-$100M revenue) drowning in manual processes
- Startups needing AI-first MVPs
- Enterprises needing custom AI agent deployments
- Companies spending $500K+/year on tasks AI can eliminate

## Conversion Strategy (Multi-Path)

### Path 1: High-Intent → Book a Strategy Call
- For visitors ready to buy
- CTA: "Book a Free Automation Audit" (not "consultation" — audits feel higher value)
- Calendly/Cal.com integration

### Path 2: Mid-Intent → Lead Magnet Capture
- "The AI Automation ROI Calculator" or "10 Workflows You Should Automate Today"
- Email capture → nurture sequence
- Builds pipeline for future closes

### Path 3: Authority → Content/Case Studies
- Case studies showing ROI ($X saved, Y hours eliminated)
- Blog/insights positioning Key as thought leader
- SEO traffic → awareness → conversion over time

## Site Architecture

```
HOME
├── Services/
│   ├── AI Agent Development
│   ├── AI Automation & Workflows
│   ├── SaaS Product Development
│   ├── Custom Software Development
│   └── Website & Web App Development
├── Case Studies/ (Results & Proof)
├── About/
├── Insights/ (Blog)
├── Contact/ (Booking Page)
└── Free Automation Audit (Landing Page)
```

## Page Priority (Build Order)
1. Homepage — first impression, conversion hub
2. Services pages — what you sell
3. About — authority and trust
4. Contact — booking integration
5. Case Studies — proof (template for now, fill as you close deals)
6. Lead Magnet Landing Page — pipeline builder

## Design Direction

### Colors (Updated)
- **Primary:** #0A0A0A (near-black — premium, authoritative)
- **Accent:** #00B4D8 (electric cyan — tech-forward, keeps brand continuity from current #04b1f4)
- **Secondary Accent:** #7C3AED (purple — premium feel, differentiator)
- **Text:** #FFFFFF on dark, #1A1A2E on light sections
- **Gradients:** Dark-to-accent for hero sections

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter or Space Grotesk (modern, clean, premium)
- **Body:** Inter (highly readable)

### Design Principles
- Dark-mode dominant (signals premium tech)
- Generous whitespace
- Bold headlines with clear hierarchy
- Animated elements on scroll (subtle, not distracting)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Speed optimized (under 3s load)

## WordPress Implementation Approach
- Theme: Astra Pro or Kadence Pro (lightweight, fast)
- Builder: Elementor Pro or Bricks Builder
- Forms: WPForms or Fluent Forms
- Booking: Calendly embed or Amelia
- SEO: RankMath Pro
- Speed: WP Rocket + Cloudflare
- Analytics: GA4 + Microsoft Clarity (heatmaps)
