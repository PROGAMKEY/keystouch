FAQ

How much does it cost?

Projects start at $10K. Most automation builds land between $10K–$50K depending on scope. The audit is free — we'll give you a number before any work starts.

How long does a typical project take?

4 weeks to first value for most automation projects. MVPs: 6–10 weeks. Full platforms: 3–6 months. We'll give you an exact timeline after Reconnaissance.

Yes. Pre-revenue to enterprise. If you have a real problem and real urgency, we're in.
