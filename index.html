The TOUCH Framework

Every agency says they "build custom solutions." We actually have a system for it. TOUCH is how we go from "my team wastes 20 hours a week on this" to "that runs itself now" — in weeks, not months, with your team trained, your data governed, and the keys in your hand.

Same framework whether we're deploying an AI agent, automating a 15-step workflow, or building a full SaaS product. The scope changes. The discipline doesn't.

Triage
